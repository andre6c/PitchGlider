What it does

Built different
from the ground up

5 Glide Curves

Exponential, ease-in, linear, ease-out, logarithmic. One knob shapes the entire character of your slide — from violin bow to trombone.

16-Voice Polyphonic

Each voice has its own independent glide. Play chords and every note slides separately. Works with any polyphonic synthesiser.

Pure MIDI

Zero audio processing. No latency, no artefacts, no coloration. Works with any synth that accepts pitchbend — hardware or software.

Live Visualiser

Watch the glide curve animate in real time. A dot moves along the curve as you play — you see exactly where the pitch is at every moment.

Legato Mode

Glide only when you hold notes and play a new one — exactly like a guitarist sliding up the neck. Off: every note glides from the last.

Direction Filter

Glide up only, down only, or both. Perfect for steel guitar (always glide up) or specific phrasing styles that only bend one way.

The core idea

Five ways to slide

The curve is everything. The same 200ms glide sounds completely different depending on how it accelerates and decelerates.
